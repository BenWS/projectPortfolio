About

Hi! My name is Ben and I'm a software developer currently located in Atlanta, Georgia. This website is a showcase of the recent projects I've worked on. Most of the technologies I've worked with in the past couple of years are those based around Java and (more recently) JavaScript languages. Proficiency in web development is what I aim to achieve in the near future.

I do have a preference for back-end development. I guess if I was charged with the task of building a car, I would rather work on the engine as opposed to the aesthetics — not to say one is more important than the other. When not building software I enjoy running, playing acoustic guitar, and hiking in the Appalachians north of town. Enjoy the website!
